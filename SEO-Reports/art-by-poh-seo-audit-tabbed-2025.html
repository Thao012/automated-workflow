Duplicate Content Patterns

Many duplicates are the same product accessible via different URLs (e.g., direct product URL vs. collection URL). This is a common Shopify issue that requires canonical tags to resolve.
Similar Product Descriptions
Products within the same series (Aubrey, Alona, The Girl) often share identical or near-identical descriptions. Each artwork needs unique content even within a series.
Collection Page Duplicates
Some collection pages may be showing identical content or products accessible through multiple collection URLs without proper canonicalisation.

Solutions for Duplicate Content

  1. Unique Descriptions: Write individual stories for each artwork, even in the same series
  2. Canonical Tags: Use canonical URLs to indicate the primary version
  3. Differentiate Framed/Unframed: Add specific content about framing options, materials, presentation
  4. Series Pages: Create overview pages for series with links to individual pieces

How to Differentiate Series Artworks

  • Size/Format: Mention how size affects the viewing experience
